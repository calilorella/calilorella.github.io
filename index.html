Would I Rather...

Would I rather be able to fly or be invisible

Well, I definitely think I would be able to get places faster with flight,
however, I think I would rather have the ability to be invisible. I think I
would choose this because just imagine being able to listen to people’s conversations
without them knowing you’re actually there. I feel like I would be able to learn a lot
by being invisible. I also think it could really come in handy in many situations.
Say I’m being chased by a serial killer and I need to disappear, I could literally
just become invisible and disappear in thin air. So overall, I would rather have the power
of invisibility over the power of flight.
